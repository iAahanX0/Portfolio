My Projects

The Illusions

Designed and developed a short narrative-driven 2D horror game using Unity and C#: Created an immersive horror experience centered around a dynamic ghost AI that adapts to player actions, featuring realistic flashlight mechanics where light affects enemy behavior. Integrated environmental puzzles, key-based progression systems, and tension-building jump scares to enhance player engagement. Used C# to script enemy pathfinding, interactive objects, event triggers, and game state transitions. Focused on atmosphere, pacing, and sound design to build suspense and deliver a compelling gameplay narrative.

Boot it to the Top

Designed and developed a punishing 2D physics-based platformer where players control a boot-shaped character on a relentless climb to the summit. Inspired by Unfair Mario and Getting Over It, the game challenges players with unpredictable obstacles, fake platforms, and deceptive paths that test reflexes and patience. Implemented physics mechanics for momentum-based high jumps using keybo. Crafted a minimalist level design that emphasizes trial-and-error learning and rewards persistence, creating a frustratingly fun and addictive gameplay loop.
